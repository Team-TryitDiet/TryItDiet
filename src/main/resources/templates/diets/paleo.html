What is a Paleo diet? A paleo diet is a dietary plan based on foods similar to what might have been eaten during the Paleolithic era, which dates from approximately 2.5 million to 10,000 years ago. Why Choose a Paleo Diet?

Choosing a Paleo diet is tailoring the diet to genetically match how humans are supposed to eat. This diet ultimately allows greater weight loss, resistance to obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular problems.
