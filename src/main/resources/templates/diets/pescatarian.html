Pescatarian

Eating the Meat Without Feet

What is a Pescatarian diet? A Pescatarian diet is an eating pattern that adds fish and seafood to a vegetarian diet.

Why Choose a Pescatarian Diet?

With this diet, there are lower risk factors to obesity and cardiovascular related diseases. The bigger health benefit is with the secret ingredient of the omega-3 fatty acids that has a plethora of benefits in regard to brain activity and health.
