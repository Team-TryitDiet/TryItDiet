Not Gonna Have a Heart Attack Here

What is a Mediterranean diet? A mediterranean diet is an eating pattern that consists of primarily consuming high amounts of vegetables, fruits, whole grains, beans, nut and seeds, and olive oil. It's important to note that there are no restrictions, but rather quantity control of how much each type of food is consumed.

Why Choose a Mediterranean Diet?

One major benefit of the Mediterranean diet, is the major reduction of risk factors associated with cardiovascular disease. This diet is also recognized by the W.H.O as a healthy and sustainable dietary pattern.
