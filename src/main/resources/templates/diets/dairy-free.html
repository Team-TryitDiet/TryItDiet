Dairy-Free
No more moo cows? No problem!

What is a dairy free diet? A dairy free diet is an eating pattern that consists of not consuming any dairy products containing milk, milk proteins (whey/casein), and/or milk sugar(lactose).

Some common dairy items

  • butter
  • cheese
  • cream
  • ice cream
  • milk
  • yogurt

Be cautious however because many products may have hidden dairy ingredients in their product. One of the best ways to identify if the product contains dairy, is to refer to the food label if possible.

Pros

  • Safe and no associated health risks
  • Alternative products/supplements to dairy are available
  • May reduce chances of developing a coronary heart disease

Cons

  • Alternatives to getting supplemental nutrients are often more expensive
  • Can be very difficult to avoid dairy, as it is used in many products, including chewing gum
  • You may not absorb all of the nutrients that you can receive from actual dairy products, versus alternatives
