Dairy-Free

No more moo cows? No problem!

What is a dairy free diet? A dairy free diet is an eating pattern that consists of not consuming any dairy products containing milk, milk proteins (whey/casein), and/or milk sugar(lactose).

Why Choose a Dairy-Free Diet?

Approximately 65% of the general population have a dairy allergen, and/or are lactose intolerant. These individuals have difficulty digesting lactose, and experience symptoms such as abdominal pain, bloating, nausea, and/or diarrhea if they consume lactose.
