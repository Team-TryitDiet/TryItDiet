What is a gluten free diet? A gluten free diet is an eating pattern that consists of not consuming gluten products containing wheat, barley, rye, and triticale.

Why Choose a Gluten-Free Diet?

A gluten-free diet was birthed as a remedy for those who have medical conditions associated with gluten such as Celiac Disease. A gluten-free diet has also been proven to improve overall health, weight loss, and increased energy.
